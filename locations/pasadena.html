What are the probate costs for Pasadena homeowners?

In Pasadena, CA, where median home values currently reach $1,350,000, statutory probate fees for a single-family residence can exceed $53,000. For deaths after April 1, 2025, any estate over $208,850 triggers mandatory court involvement. A Living Trust allows your heirs to bypass the Pasadena Superior Court entirely, keeping your private financial affairs off the public record.

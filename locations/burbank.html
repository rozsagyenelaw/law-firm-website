Protecting Entertainment Assets in the Media District

Burbank is home to Disney, Warner Bros, and Nickelodeon. Many of our clients are industry professionals with unique estate needs. Unlike a standard bank account, "creative assets" like residuals, royalties, and intellectual property require specific handling in a California Living Trust.

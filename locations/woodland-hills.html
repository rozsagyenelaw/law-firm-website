Navigating the Stanley Mosk Courthouse Backlog

While Woodland Hills is in the West Valley, all formal probate administration is centralized at the Stanley Mosk Courthouse (111 N. Hill St, Los Angeles, CA 90012). The current backlog in LA County means even simple petitions can take 12 to 24 months to resolve. During this window, your home in 91364 or 91367 cannot be sold to cover debts or inheritance without a court order.

Proposition 19 & Property Tax Defense

Woodland Hills real estate has seen significant appreciation, especially south of the boulevard. Under California Proposition 19, if your heirs inherit your primary residence and do not make it their own primary residence within 12 months, the annual tax bill could jump from $5,000 to $18,000 or more. Our Living Trust packages include flexible distribution language designed to help preserve your low tax base for your children whenever legally possible.

Incapacity Planning for Professionals

Beyond death planning, a comprehensive estate plan includes Durable Power of Attorney and Advance Healthcare Directives. This is critical for Woodland Hills professionals who manage high-value investments or businesses. If you become incapacitated due to illness or injury without these documents, your family would be forced to file for a public and expensive Court Conservatorship. A trust-based plan ensures your hand-picked successor steps in immediately with zero court involvement.

The Crisis of Probate in Los Angeles County

Serving as an executor in Los Angeles is a monumental task. If you pass away in LA without an estate plan, your family must deal with the Los Angeles County Superior Court Probate Division. The primary hub for these cases is the Stanley Mosk Courthouse. Due to the sheer volume of cases, the probate process currently takes 12 to 24 months.

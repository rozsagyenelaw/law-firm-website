Why is a Living Trust mandatory for Tarzana homeowners?

Navigating the Stanley Mosk Courthouse: The Grieving Burden

Although Tarzana is in the heart of the Valley, all probate matters are centralized at the Stanley Mosk Courthouse (111 N. Hill St, Los Angeles, CA 90012). The commute to Downtown LA and the court's 18-month backlog create a heavy emotional and financial burden on executors. As your local counsel, we leverage the court's electronic systems to minimize delays and handle the bureaucratic hurdles of LA County Superior Court administration.

Prop 19 Defense: Protecting the Family Home

Properties South of the Boulevard have appreciated massively over the last 20 years. Proposition 19 (Family Home Protection Act) significantly changed how your children inherit your tax base. If your home is not their primary residence, the property tax will be reassessed to market value. We implement specialized trust language to give your heirs the flexibility needed to keep the family home without the "tax shock" that forces many Tarzana families to sell.

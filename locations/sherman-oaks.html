Navigating the Stanley Mosk Courthouse Backlog

Sherman Oaks probate matters are handled at the Stanley Mosk Courthouse (111 N Hill St, Los Angeles, CA 90012), the central probate court for all of Los Angeles County. Currently, the court is navigating a massive case volume, with 12 to 24-month timelines for closing even simple estates.

During this period, your Sherman Oaks real estate is effectively frozen. It cannot be sold or refinanced without a court order, which can take months to obtain. A Living Trust allows your successor trustee to take control of your property within days, ensuring family financial stability and the ability to act quickly on market opportunities.

Specialized Planning for the "Executive Lifestyle"

We serve many clients who hold stock options, RSUs, and complex brokerage accounts. These assets require precise "Trust Funding" to ensure they do not trigger a probate filing upon death. We provide a tailored Funding Roadmap for every client, assisting with the retitling of your 91423 home and coordinating your beneficiary designations across all retirement accounts (401k/403b) to ensure your legacy remains in your family's hands, not the court's.

The Heggstad Petition: Saving Local Homes

If a loved one died with a trust but forgot to move their Sherman Oaks house into it, we can often file a Heggstad Petition (Probate Code § 850). This allows us to ask the judge to recognize the house as part of the trust retroactively. This is one of our firm's core specialties, and we have successfully saved dozens of local families from the full probate process using this procedure.

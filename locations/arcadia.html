Why is a Living Trust essential for Arcadia families?

With Arcadia median home prices exceeding $1,800,000, nearly every residence in neighborhoods like Santa Anita Oaks or Baldwin Stocker is subject to mandatory probate under California law. Without a Living Trust, your family faces an 18-month court backlog and statutory fees of approximately $62,000. A trust allows for immediate, private transfer of these high-value assets without the scrutiny of the public record.

International Asset Coordination: China, Taiwan, & Beyond

A unique challenge for Arcadia residents is the management of foreign assets. If you own property, business interests, or accounts in China, Taiwan, or Hong Kong, your U.S. estate plan must be coordinated to prevent double taxation or complex probate proceedings in multiple countries. While a California Living Trust handles your local real estate, we provide the legal guidance needed to disclose and structure your global legacy correctly.

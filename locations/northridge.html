Navigating the Stanley Mosk Courthouse

When a Northridge resident passes away without a trust, their family must deal with the Los Angeles Superior Court. All probate matters are centralized at the Stanley Mosk Courthouse (111 N Hill St, Los Angeles, CA 90012). The commute from the North Valley to Downtown LA, combined with the court's 12-24 month backlog, creates a heavy emotional and financial burden on executors.

Navigating LA County probate rules requires local expertise. A single filing error can delay an inheritance by 3 to 6 months. As your Northridge probate lawyer, we handle all electronic filings, court appearances, and creditor notifications, ensuring your family isn't stuck in the lengthy Stanley Mosk backlog.

Prop 19 Defense: Protecting Your Family Home

Because many Northridge properties have been held for decades, they carry an extremely low tax base. Under California Proposition 19, if your heirs inherit your primary residence and do not make it their own primary residence within 12 months, the annual tax bill will jump to market value. On an $1.1M home, this can mean a tax jump from $3,000 to $15,000 per year. Our trusts include the specific legal language needed to give your heirs the best chance at tax preservation.

Prop 19 Defense for Ultra-High-Equity Real Estate

For estates in The Flats or Mulholland Estates, Proposition 19 remains a primary financial threat. In 2025, the inflation-adjusted exclusion cap is $1,044,586 over the taxable base. If an executor fails to coordinate the heir's residency and the Assessor's filing within one year, a luxury property tax bill could jump from $15,000 to over $100,000 annually. We manage these filings with the LA County Assessor concurrently with the probate to ensure generational wealth preservation.

Proposition 19: The Property Tax Threat for Glendale Heirs

If you are administering a trust that includes a primary residence in Glendale, you are on a strict clock. Under California Proposition 19, if the children do not move into the parent's home as their primary residence within 12 months, the property tax will be reassessed to market value. On a $1.2M Glendale home, this could mean annual taxes jumping from $3,000 to $15,000 permanently. We specialize in "Prop 19 Defense," ensuring all filings with the LA County Assessor are completed accurately to protect the family legacy.

The Heggstad Rescue: When Assets are Left Out

Did you find a Glendale property that was never formally transferred into the trust? Before you file for a full 18-month probate, let us evaluate if we can file a Heggstad Petition (Probate Code § 850). We have successfully rescued millions in Glendale real estate by asking the court to retroactively "fund" the trust, saving heirs significant time and money.

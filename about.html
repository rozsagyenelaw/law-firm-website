Protecting California Families for Over 25 Years

Since 2001, the Law Offices of Rozsa Gyene has been dedicated to providing comprehensive estate planning and probate services to families throughout Southern California. With over 25 years of experience, Attorney Rozsa Gyene brings deep expertise and compassionate guidance to every client relationship.

A graduate of Southwestern Law School, Attorney Rozsa Gyene is licensed by the State Bar of California (Bar #208356) and the Massachusetts State Bar, and admitted to the United States District Court, Central District of California. She has built a reputation for excellence in estate planning, probate administration, conservatorships, and guardianships. Her practice focuses on creating personalized solutions that protect assets, minimize taxes, and ensure clients' wishes are carried out.

My Mission: Making Estate Planning Accessible to Everyone

What motivates me most in my practice is helping families who have been priced out of quality legal services. I've witnessed too many hardworking, low-income families struggle without proper estate planning simply because they couldn't afford traditional attorney fees. Every family deserves the peace of mind that comes with knowing their loved ones will be protected and their assets will be distributed according to their wishes‚Äîregardless of their income level.

That's why I've built my practice around affordability without compromising quality. By streamlining processes and keeping overhead low, I can offer comprehensive living trusts and estate planning documents at prices that working families can actually afford. There's nothing more rewarding than helping a family secure their future and knowing that financial barriers didn't stand in their way. Estate planning shouldn't be a luxury reserved for the wealthy‚Äîit's a fundamental right that every California family should have access to.

Areas of Expertise

Estate Planning

Conservatorships

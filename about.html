Protecting California Families for Over 25 Years

Since 2001, the Law Offices of Rozsa Gyene has been dedicated to providing comprehensive estate planning and probate services to families throughout Southern California. With over 25 years of experience, Attorney Rozsa Gyene brings deep expertise and compassionate guidance to every client relationship.

Licensed by the State Bar of California (Bar #208356), Rozsa Gyene has built a reputation for excellence in estate planning, probate administration, conservatorships, and guardianships. Her practice focuses on creating personalized solutions that protect assets, minimize taxes, and ensure clients' wishes are carried out.

Located in the heart of Glendale at 450 N Brand Blvd, Suite 600, the firm serves clients throughout Los Angeles County and the surrounding areas, offering both in-person and virtual consultations to accommodate every client's needs.

Areas of Expertise

Estate Planning

Comprehensive planning including living trusts, wills, powers of attorney, and advance healthcare directives tailored to your unique needs.

Probate Administration

Expert guidance through the probate process, from filing petitions to final distribution, making a difficult time easier for families.

Conservatorships

Compassionate assistance establishing conservatorships for adults who cannot manage their own affairs due to incapacity.

Guardianships

Legal representation for families seeking guardianship of minor children when parents are unable to provide care.

Asset Protection

Strategic planning to protect your assets from creditors, lawsuits, and unnecessary taxation while preserving wealth for future generations.

Trust Administration

Guidance for trustees in properly administering trusts, including asset management, distributions, and tax compliance.

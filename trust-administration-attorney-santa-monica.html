Prop 19 Defense for Coastal Santa Monica Real Estate

Santa Monica property appreciation is some of the highest in the country. Under California Proposition 19 (updated for 2025 with an adjusted exclusion cap of $1,044,586), if a child inherits a parent's home and does not move in within one year, the tax base will be reassessed to market value. For a home in North of Montana, this can mean an annual tax hike of $30,000 or more. We handle the critical exclusion filings to ensure the family's low tax base is preserved whenever legally possible.

Protecting Yourself from Personal Liability & Surcharges

Successor trustees are held to the "Prudent Investor" standard. If you fail to invest assets wisely, miss a mandatory creditor notice, or skip the final trust accounting, beneficiaries can petition the court to "surcharge" you—making you personally responsible for any perceived financial loss. Our firm acts as your legal counsel, documenting every step of the trust administration process to shield your personal assets.

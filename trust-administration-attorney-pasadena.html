Pasadena properties have often been held for decades, resulting in an extremely low tax base. Under California Proposition 19, if a successor trustee fails to file the correct exemption paperwork within one year of the settlor's death, the heirs could face a property tax increase of $20,000 or more per year. Avoiding Personal Liability & Surcharges

Successor trustees are held to the "Prudent Investor" standard. If you make unauthorized investments, fail to pay valid taxes, or mismanage the trust administration process, you can be held personally liable. Beneficiaries have the right to petition the court to "surcharge" a trustee for mismanagement. Our firm provides the legal oversight needed to protect your personal assets while you fulfill your role.

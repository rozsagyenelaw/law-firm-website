Stanley Mosk Successor Trustee Checklist

Essential Steps for Burbank Executors Navigating the Downtown LA Probate Division

About This Checklist: When a loved one passes away and you've been named as executor or successor trustee, knowing the proper steps can save months of delays and thousands in costs. This checklist covers the essential filings and procedures at Stanley Mosk Courthouse (111 N. Hill Street, Los Angeles).

Note: All Burbank probate matters are handled at Stanley Mosk Courthouse, the central probate division for Los Angeles County.

1 Immediate Steps (First 48-72 Hours)

  • Locate the original trust document and/or will - Check safe deposit boxes, home safes, and with the decedent's attorney
  • Obtain certified copies of the death certificate - Order at least 10-15 copies from the funeral home or LA County Registrar
  • Secure the decedent's property - Change locks if necessary, secure valuables, and document everything with photos
  • Notify immediate family members - Inform all heirs and beneficiaries named in the trust or will
  • Contact an experienced probate attorney - Get guidance before taking any major actions
Important Deadline: You must file the original will with the court within 30 days of learning of the death (California Probate Code Section 8200).

2 Trust Administration (If Trust Exists)

  • Send Notification to Beneficiaries (Form DE-175) - Required within 60 days of death or 60 days of becoming trustee
  • Obtain a Tax ID Number (EIN) - Required for the trust from the IRS (can be done online)
  • Open a trust bank account - Use the new EIN for all trust financial transactions
  • Inventory all trust assets - Document real estate, bank accounts, investments, personal property
  • Get appraisals for real property - Needed for date-of-death values and Step-up in Basis
  • Pay outstanding debts and final expenses - Medical bills, funeral costs, credit cards, mortgages
  • File the decedent's final tax return - Due April 15th of the year following death
Pro Tip: Even with a trust, some assets may require a Heggstad Petition if they weren't properly transferred into the trust during the decedent's lifetime. This is common with Burbank real estate.

3 Probate Court Filings (If Required)

  • File Petition for Probate (DE-111) - Opens the probate case at Stanley Mosk
  • Pay filing fee - Currently $435-$465 for initial petition
  • Publish Notice to Creditors - Required in a newspaper of general circulation
  • File Inventory and Appraisal (DE-160) - Due within 4 months of appointment
  • Request Letters Testamentary - Certified letters grant you authority to act
  • Wait for creditor claims period - 4 months from date of notice publication
  • File Final Accounting and Distribution - After all debts paid, distribute to heirs

4 Special Considerations for Burbank Estates

  • Entertainment Industry Residuals - Contact SAG-AFTRA, DGA, WGA for residual transfers
  • Guild Pension Benefits - File survivor benefit claims with appropriate guilds
  • Studio Employment Records - Request employment records for asset valuation
  • Proposition 19 Compliance - File for property tax exclusion within 1 year of transfer
  • Media District Properties - Special zoning considerations for commercial properties
  • Rancho District Horse Properties - Unique appraisal requirements for equestrian zoning
Warning: Missing the Proposition 19 deadline can result in property tax reassessment at current market value, potentially increasing annual taxes by thousands of dollars.

5 Final Distribution Checklist

  • Obtain tax clearance - Ensure all estate taxes are paid or waived
  • Prepare distribution schedule - Document exactly what each beneficiary receives
  • Transfer real property deeds - Record new deeds with LA County Recorder
  • Distribute personal property - Follow the trust/will instructions precisely
  • Obtain receipts from beneficiaries - Document all distributions received
  • File closing documents with court - If probate was required
  • Retain records for 5+ years - Keep copies of all documents and receipts

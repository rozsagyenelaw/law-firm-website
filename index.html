Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a living trust?

If you own property in California, a trust avoids the 12-18 month probate delay and saves your family thousands in court fees.

How much does probate cost?

California statutory fees start at 4% of the estate value. A $1M home means $46,000 in combined attorney and executor fees.

Where are Glendale probate cases heard?

All Glendale, Burbank, and Pasadena probate cases are heard at the Stanley Mosk Courthouse in Downtown Los Angeles.

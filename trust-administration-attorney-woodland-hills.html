Proposition 19: Protecting the Woodland Hills Family Home

In 2025, Proposition 19 is the single largest property tax threat for West Valley heirs. If a child inherits a parent's primary residence in Woodland West or Corbin Palms and fails to move in and file for the exclusion within one year, the tax base will be reassessed to market value. For many local families, this can mean a tax hike from $3,000 to over $18,000 per year. We coordinate with the LA County Assessor to ensure all "Family Home Protection" filings are submitted accurately and on time.
